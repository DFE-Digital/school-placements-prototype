Sharing the placements you're looking for

The service can estimate your need for placements. These can be shared with schools if you choose to.

This is based on the number of candidates that are successful and the number of placements you offer each candidate. A successful candidate in Manage teacher training applications is one with a status of shortlisted, offered, or recruited.

You can change how this number is calculated from your settings at any time.
